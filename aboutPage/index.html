Our Story

Who We Are

Fixing PPE Waste – For Good

The PPE industry has been built on waste and inefficiency. Circular Supply is here to change that.

We are Canada's first done-for-you PPE recycling and remanufacturing program, designed to eliminate PPE waste, reduce costs, and drive real sustainability – not just greenwashing.

As a Métis-owned, impact-driven business, we're closing the loop on PPE waste, ensuring that what was once disposable becomes valuable again. Our approach isn't just about sustainability – it's about efficiency, cost savings, and responsibility.

Our Mission

Circular Supply is redefining PPE by making sustainability the smart choice for businesses. We exist to eliminate PPE waste, streamline ESG compliance, and help companies create measurable impact – without disrupting their operations or increasing costs. Our mission is to make full-circle PPE the industry standard by proving that sustainability and profitability can go hand in hand.

Empowering future generations

At Circular Supply, sustainability isn't a corporate buzzword—it's the foundation of everything we do. We help businesses meet Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) regulations and align with ESG goals by providing a seamless, cost-effective, and impact-driven approach to PPE waste.

We're not just reducing waste—we're eliminating it. By designing for circularity, we're closing the loop on PPE disposal, transforming waste into new products, and helping businesses achieve true sustainability—without added complexity.
